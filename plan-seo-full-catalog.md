## SEO Requirements Per Product
1. **Title**: SEO-optimized, corrected color hierarchy, searchable fabric terms
2. **Meta Description**: 150-160 chars, keyword-rich, CTA
3. **Description**: 200-400 words, craftsmanship storytelling, occasion guidance
4. **Keywords**: 10-15 relevant keywords including long-tail
5. **Image Alt Text**: Descriptive with color, fabric, angle
6. **Q&A**: 2-3 FAQ pairs for AI search
7. **Schema**: Product, Breadcrumb, FAQ structured data
