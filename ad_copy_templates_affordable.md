# LuxeMia Ad Copy Templates: Affordable Boutique Positioning

## Overview

This document provides updated ad copy templates for LuxeMia, pivoting from "luxury" to a "middle-class affordable boutique" brand. The copy emphasizes value, fair pricing, and accessible elegance while maintaining a professional and trustworthy tone.

## 3. Brand Voice Guidelines (Affordable Boutique)
*   **Keywords:** Affordable, Fair Prices, Accessible Elegance, Value, Quality-Inspected, Diaspora-Focused.
*   **Avoid:** "Ultra-luxury," "Couture prices," "Exclusive high-end."
*   **Emphasize:** Direct-to-consumer savings, middle-class budget friendly, transparent pricing, and cultural connection.
