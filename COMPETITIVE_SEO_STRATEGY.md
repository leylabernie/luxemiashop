## WHAT COMPETITORS DO THAT LUXEMIA DOESN'T

### 1. Transactional Title Format (ALL 3 competitors)
- **Kalki**: "Buy Purple Banarasi Brocade Lehenga Set | KALKI Fashion Global"
- **Cbazaar**: "Shop Red Silk Zari Embroidered Sequins Lehenga Wedding Wear Online at Best Price | Cbazaar"
- **Utsav**: "Buy [Product] Online : [SKU] - Utsav Fashion"
- **LuxeMia CURRENT**: "Pearl White Silk Lehenga Choli with Maroon Embroidered Dupatta | Lehenga | LuxeMia"
- **LuxeMia SHOULD BE**: "Buy Pearl White Silk Lehenga Choli with Maroon Embroidered Dupatta Online | LuxeMia"

### 2. Full Product Schema with Offer + ShippingDetails + MerchantReturnPolicy (Kalki)
Kalki has the MOST complete Product schema. LuxeMia has shipping and return policy functions but they need to be wired into the Product schema output.

### 3. ItemList Schema on Collection Pages (Kalki)
Kalki uses ItemList schema on collection pages with up to 50 products listed. This enables:
- Product carousel rich snippets in SERPs
- Google Shopping free listings
- Better collection page indexing

### 4. Real Bride / Customer Story Content (Kalki)
Kalki's blog has "Real Brides" section with actual customer wedding photos. This is:
- Unique content (UGC) that can't be duplicated
- Emotional engagement
- Natural backlink magnet
- E-E-A-T signal (Experience)

### 5. Product Specification Table (Kalki + Cbazaar)
Both show structured product specs:
- Style No / SKU
- Color
- Fabric
- Work/Embroidery Type
- Pack Contains (Lehenga, Choli, Dupatta)
- Occasion
- Care Instructions

### 6. Expandable Collection Editorial Content (Kalki)
Kalki has 60-80 word expandable SEO paragraphs on collection pages with "Read More" buttons. This provides:
- Fresh content for search engines
- Keyword-rich context
- No design clutter (expandable)

### 7. Occasion-Based Subcategories (ALL 3)
- **Wedding**: Bridal Lehengas, Reception Lehengas, Sangeet Lehengas
- **Pre-Wedding**: Engagement Lehengas, Mehendi Lehengas, Haldi Lehengas
- **Festive**: Diwali, Eid, Navratri, Durga Puja
- **Other**: Party Wear, Festive Wear, Casual

### 8. Weekly Blog Publishing (Cbazaar + Kalki)
- Cbazaar: Weekly posts, 40+ keyword tags
- Kalki: Real brides, celebrity style, trend reports
- UtsavFashion: Showcase + Concept pages (not traditional blog)

### 9. Bilingual SEO / Hindi Keywords (UtsavFashion)
Utsav uses Hindi script in titles: "लहंगा" alongside "Lehenga"
- Captures Hindi-speaking searchers
- Cultural authenticity signal

### 10. Product Count in Breadcrumbs (Cbazaar)
"Home > Lehengas (2,450+)" - shows scale and freshness
